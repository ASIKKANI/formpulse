# FormPulse

## The Future of Conversational Data Collection

FormPulse is a modern, AI-powered platform that reimagines how businesses collect feedback and survey responses. Instead of relying on static, tedious forms that feel like homework, FormPulse turns any survey or input schema into a warm, natural chat conversation.

---

### The Problem
Traditional online forms (like Google Forms or Typeform) suffer from significant limitations:
*   **Low Engagement:** Respondents face walls of text inputs and checkboxes, leading to high drop-off rates.
*   **Shallow Data:** Vague, one-word answers (e.g., "it was okay") cannot be clarified, leaving researchers with uninformative data.
*   **Rigid Structures:** Static forms cannot adapt to the respondent's emotions, sentiment, or specific issues in real-time.

---

### The Solution: FormPulse
FormPulse converts standard structured schemas into a dynamic, two-way conversational flow. Respondents complete forms by chatting with an intelligent, empathetic agent on Web, WhatsApp, or Slack.

#### 1. Dynamic Probing (Sub-questions)
When a user provides a brief, vague, or negative response (e.g., *"The dashboard lagged"*), FormPulse does not simply move to the next field. It dynamically asks **exactly one** spontaneous follow-up question (e.g., *"Oh no, where did you notice the lag most—during file uploads or loading the dashboard?"*). Once the user answers, the AI gracefully transitions back to the main survey schema.

#### 2. Empathetic Active Listening
FormPulse integrates micro-expressions and conversational anchors based on sentiment analysis (e.g., *"I hear you, database setups are always tricky!"*). This validation builds rapport and encourages respondents to share longer, more detailed feedback.

#### 3. Multi-Modal Inputs (OCR & RAG)
Respondents can submit more than just text. They can upload screenshots, logs, PDFs, and links. The system automatically runs Optical Character Recognition (OCR) and retrieves relevant context using a vector database (RAG) to process attachments in real-time.

#### 4. Automated AI Cohort Analytics
Behind the scenes, FormPulse clusters response transcripts mathematically. It automatically extracts sentiment, groups users into cohorts (e.g., "Pricing Concerns" vs. "Performance Issues"), and maps them onto a 2D interactive coordinate plane for analysis.

---

### Why the Name?
*   **Form:** Represents the structured, goal-oriented nature of data collection.
*   **Pulse:** Represents life, activity, and real-time interaction. The forms are "alive," matching the rhythm and flow of a human conversation.

---

### Key Use Cases
*   **B2B User Research:** Gather detailed qualitative feedback on product features and performance.
*   **Customer Support Ticketing:** Qualify issues with files, screenshots, and logs before routing to human agents.
*   **Lead Qualification:** Collect user requirements through casual, conversational intake flows.
